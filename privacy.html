Support & Privacy

Movie Face Swap

This page explains how the app works, how we handle your photos and data, and how to contact us if you need help.

About the App

Discover movies and slip your own face into iconic posters and scenes.

  • Browse photos and posters from movies and TV shows.
  • Swap your face (or your friends’) into characters and scenes.
  • Edit the result with frames, filters, crop and rotate.
  • Save, share or revisit your previous creations anytime.

Privacy & Data

We respect your privacy. The app uses your photos only for creating face-swap images and related edits.

  • Your photos are chosen by you from your device or camera.
  • Face-swap processing may be done on-device or securely on our servers, only to generate your result image.
  • We do not use your personal photos to train public AI models or sell them to third parties.
  • Our app accesses user-selected photos to perform face-swap effects. We do not collect or store biometric identifiers or facial templates.
  • Face processing is performed on-device or temporarily on our secure servers only to generate the final image. All temporary data is deleted immediately after processing.
  • We do not share facial data with any third parties. Only the output image is saved if the user requests it.
  • You can delete your creations from within the app at any time. If removed, they are no longer visible in the app.
Important: If you share a face-swap image to social media or messaging apps, that image is then handled under those services’ own privacy policies.

Face Data Retention

  • Our app does not retain face data.
  • With your input face, we only use it to swap face into new picture without saving or retaining in any server.
  • Your face photo is saved only in your phone's local storage for next usage if you want
  • Any facial landmarks or temporary data used for generating the face-swap result are processed only at runtime and are immediately deleted after the output image is created.
  • No face data is stored, saved, or kept on our servers or on the user’s device after processing.

Movie Content & Copyright

Movie posters and stills shown in the app are used only to help you discover films and create personal face-swap images.

  • The app does not stream or provide full-length movies.
  • All movie titles, posters, characters and related artwork are the property of their respective owners and rights holders.
  • This app is a fan tool for creative photo edits and does not claim any ownership over movie content.

Analytics & Usage

To keep improving the app, we may collect limited anonymous usage data, such as:

  • Which features are used most often.
  • Basic device and app version information.
  • Crash reports to fix bugs and improve stability.

This data is used only to understand overall trends and enhance user experience, not to personally identify you.

Advertising

The app may show ads to keep the service free. Advertising partners may use anonymous identifiers to show relevant ads, following their own privacy policies.

On iOS, if we use tracking for ads, the system will ask for your permission first via the standard App Tracking Transparency prompt.
